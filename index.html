OUR STORY

In 2005, the company “Fanam-Cinema” began to create a cinema that meets the world standards for the quality of film screening and the service provided. The bias was made on the development of the most popular concept of a multiplex cinema, combined with a wide range of additional services and modern design. The financial partner for the implementation of a large-scale and historic event was the JSCB Almazergienbank OJSC.
